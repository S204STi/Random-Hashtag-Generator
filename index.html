Random Hastag Generator

This is an early project of mine from my time in Galvanize, a webdev bootcamp. The inspiration for it was seeing friends on social media using insanely long chains of hashtags on nearly every post, and wanting to have a quick and easy way to mock them into a state of deep depression. This website was built using primarily HTML and JS, with some Bootstrap implementation. The APIs I'm using to generate the random text are Randomtext.me, and Baconipsum.com to whom I am deeply grateful. Check out their stuff for your own projects. -S204STi
